This paper introduces C-Eval, a comprehensive Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. The evaluation covers multiple disciplines and difficulty levels to provide a thorough assessment of model capabilities in Chinese language understanding and reasoning.

**Key Contributions:**
1. Development of a multi-level, multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite
2. Comprehensive coverage of diverse knowledge domains
3. Framework for systematic evaluation of foundation models in Chinese

C-Eval addresses the need for robust evaluation benchmarks for Chinese language models, providing a standardized framework for assessing model performance across different knowledge domains and difficulty levels. The suite enables meaningful comparisons between models and tracking of progress in Chinese NLP.
