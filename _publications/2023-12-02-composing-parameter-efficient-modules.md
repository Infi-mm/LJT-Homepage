This paper explores composing parameter-efficient modules using arithmetic operations for efficient adaptation of large language models. The approach enables flexible combination of different adaptation strategies while maintaining parameter efficiency.

**Key Contributions:**
1. Framework for composing parameter-efficient modules with arithmetic operations
2. Flexible adaptation strategies through module composition
3. Maintenance of parameter efficiency in model adaptation

The research addresses the challenge of adapting large language models to specific tasks while minimizing the number of trainable parameters. By enabling arithmetic composition of different adaptation modules, the approach provides greater flexibility in model customization while preserving the efficiency benefits of parameter-efficient fine-tuning methods.
