This paper investigates the perception bottleneck in Vision-Language Models (VLMs) for chart understanding tasks. We identify key limitations in current VLMs when processing complex visual information from charts and propose solutions to address these challenges.

**Key Contributions:**
1. Identification of perception bottlenecks in VLMs for chart understanding
2. Analysis of visual information processing limitations
3. Proposed solutions to enhance chart comprehension capabilities

The research focuses on understanding why current VLMs struggle with chart interpretation tasks despite their success in other vision-language domains. We examine the underlying architectural limitations and propose modifications to improve performance.
